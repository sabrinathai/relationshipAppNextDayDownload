Daily Experiences in Dual Career Couples Study App Re-Installation Download Page

Please download the app below if you have started the study but entered your work and/or sleep time incorrectly

iPhone/iPad version 1.0.4
Install iOS version 1.0.4

Follow the instructions on the page. If you need help with installing the app, click Help on the sidebar. Or follow the instructions below.

After downloading the app, go to Settings > General.
Scroll down to VPN & Device Management, and then click on it.
You will see Embodied Social Cognition.
Click on Embodied Social Cognition and then click on Trust "Embodied Social Cognition".


Android version 1.0.3
Install Android version 1.0.3

1. After downloading the app, go to Settings > Security.
2. Check the option "Unknown sources".
3. Tap OK on the prompt message.
4. Select "Trust".
